LCD and knobs case
===

simple LCD case, to be mounted on top of prusa i3, next to the filament (spool) holder.
case has a room for LCD, SD card reader, reset button and menu knob.

to assemble it also requires:
 - six 5mm nuts
 - twelve 5mm washers
 - six 5mm screws
 - six 3mm nuts
 - six 3mm washers
 - six 3mm screws

print time:
 - front: ~3:15h
 - back:  ~2h
 - knob:  ~0:20h
